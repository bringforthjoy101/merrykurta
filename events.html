Stormzy HITH The World Tour

Events Description

Set to be one of the most highly anticipated tours of 2020, Stormzy has announced a huge 55-date ‘H.I.T.H. World Tour’. With shows spanning across Europe, Asia, Australia, New Zealand, North America, UK & Ireland and Africa, the tour marks Stormzy’s biggest live offering to date. Kicking off the tour in Dubai on February 7th and concluding in Lagos on October 10, 2020, the tour will also see him make a solo debut at the Balomoral Convention Centre,Federal Palace Hotel V.I as he ends the world tour in Africa! The H.I.T.H World Tour in Lagos is brought to you by the multiple award winning and arguably the number one urban radio station in Africa, THE BEAT 99.9FM. Don't be told,get your tickets here now!
